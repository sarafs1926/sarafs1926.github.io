Sara's Webpage

Hi, I'm Sara and I like to study fundamental physics. I develop hardware and software that help me do this. Right now I work on the Super-Kamiokande and Hyper-Kamiokande collaborations at Boston University. I am searching for dinucleon and nucleon decay and I am helping to develop front-end hardware for Hyper-K using these ASICs . I'm also one of the Super-K high voltage experts, which means I fix HV-related problems in our detector. I have also worked on upgrades for the ATLAS muon spectrometer, and I made a website to monitor its detector elements.

I've also done some other fun things, like attend International Neutrino Summer School at Fermilab.

I'm still developing this webpage, so stay tuned. I plan to upload pictures, my CV, and some cool physics resources you might like.
