Sara's Webpage

Hi, I'm Sara and I like to study fundamental physics. I develop hardware and software that help me do this. I'm a second year Ph.D. student at Princeton currently working on quantum computing research in Andrew Houck's lab. Before that I worked primarily on the Super-Kamiokande and Hyper-Kamiokande collaborations at Boston University.

"Large uncertainties are inherent at every level of engineering. Many of the uncertainties are small with respect to other uncertainties and therefore may be ignored. There are many balancing acts we should perform; in this case, balancing ignorance against knowledge."
